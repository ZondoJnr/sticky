Hobbies

Playing the keyboard

Learning new songs on the piano was an exciting journey for me. I remember spending hours in my room with my keyboard.

Winning achievements for my school in choir competitions was a proud childhood achievement. It motivated me to master the keys!

Drawing

I loved sketching superheroes with bold, exaggerated features. It sparked my imagination and creativity!

Accidentally turning my sketches into a mini comic became a funny family moment when I showed my masterpiece.

Toys

Recreating battles from my favorite TV shows with action figures was my way of bringing those stories to life.

One toy had a loose arm, but it didn’t stop me from boldly declaring it my most trusted sidekick.

Constructing imaginative cities with colorful blocks was pure joy. It felt like being an architect in my tiny world.

I once built a castle so tall it almost reached the table – an epic success!
