About Keyboard Playing

A keyboard player is a musician who specializes in playing keyboard instruments, such as pianos, synthesizers, organs, and digital workstations.
These instruments are incredibly versatile and can produce a wide range of sounds, making keyboard players essential in many musical genres like classical, jazz, gospel, and 'amapiano' music.
Coming from a musical family, I had an inclination towards musical instruments from an early age. I have been playing keyboards since 2007.
For me, music is a way of life. I play at church for worship, I play in events for entertainment, and most importantly I play at home for fun!
And yes, I am a devout Roland fan!
